Membership Levels

Non Profit

Cost: For non profit organizations and there is no fee.

Benefits:

  • Access to basic resources and information.
  • Subscription to newsletters or updates.
  • Invitation to general events or meetings.

Bronze Membership

Cost: Slightly higher than the non-profit level.

Benefits:

  • All benefits of the non-profit level.
  • Additional access to certain training sessions or workshops.
  • Recognition on the organization's website or publications.
  • Discounted rates for specific events or services.

Silver Membership

Cost: Slightly higher than the Bronze level.

Benefits:

  • All benefits of the Bronze level.
  • Priority registration for events or workshops.
  • Featured spotlight positions on the organization's website or social media.
  • Enhanced networking opportunities, such as exclusive meet-and-greets.

Gold Membership

Cost: Slightly higher than the Silver level.

Benefits:

  • All benefits of the Silver level.
  • Exclusive access to VIP events or private networking sessions.
  • Personalized advertising opportunities, such as dedicated email blasts or sponsored content.
  • Special recognition at major events or in organizational materials.
